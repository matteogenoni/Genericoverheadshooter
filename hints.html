HINTS


Remember, there will almost always be an enemy on screen, so keep that “shoot” button pressed down. It doesn’t slow you down and you’ve got an endless amount of ammo, so as long as you’re moving, you should be blastin’ as well.

Once the enemies touch you, it’s game over and right back to the beginning. As long as you don’t let them outmaneuver you, everything should be fine.

You’re faster than the enemies, but they outnumber you to 1000/1. Take a mental image of the map around you, and make sure you never get cornered.

The main inhabitants of the base, the slimes, seem weak, but watch out! They’ve got something on their side that you don’t wanna meet.
